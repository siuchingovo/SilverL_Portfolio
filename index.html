SmileVerse

#Virtual Reailty #Facial Tracking #Emotion Contagion
About

SmileVerse initially developed for the HTC Vive Pro Eye, the project was later ported to the Meta Quest Pro, increasing accessibility. This project combines visual and auditory feedback devices, utilizing VR technology and the VIVE Facial Tracker. It detects user expressions, enabling a collective emotional infection experience through XR and 5G technology. External participants can join the VR story via smartphone screens, enhancing the immersive experience.

* This project was funded by the 2022 Future Content Prototype Development program of the Taiwan Creative Content Agency (TAICCA).

SiLIDcon

#Internet of Thing #Food Security #OpenCV
About

SiLIDcon utilizes humidity and alcohol sensors to conduct real-time testing of stored food and transmits the data to the cloud for continuous monitoring. If alcohol substances and humidity exceed permitted levels, users are notified via Line or other social apps. OpenCV is used to recognize food and store it in a database, setting expiration dates based on the U.S. government's "Food Safety Chart." The device notifies users via Line to consume food promptly, and also employs red, yellow, and green lights to indicate the food's condition. The device's principle is based on the fact that food decay releases alcohol or acetic acid, which can be measured by alcohol sensors. Once levels surpass the standard, users are notified not to consume the food.
